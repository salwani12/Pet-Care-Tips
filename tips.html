Pet Care Tips

Dogs

Tip 1: Take your dog for two daily walks to keep them happy and healthy.

Tip 2: Brush their fur weekly to reduce shedding and avoid matting.

Did You Know?
Dogs' tapetum lucidum layer allows them to see better in the dark than humans.

Cats

Tip 1: Use a scratching post to protect your furniture and your cat's claws.

Tip 2: Schedule regular vet visits to monitor common illnesses.

Did You Know?
Cats sleep for about 70% of their lives.

Birds

Tip 1: Provide toys like puzzles and chewable materials to keep your bird mentally stimulated.

Tip 2: Clean their cage regularly and provide fresh water daily.

Did You Know?
Parrots can mimic human speech and sounds, thanks to their advanced vocal learning skills.
